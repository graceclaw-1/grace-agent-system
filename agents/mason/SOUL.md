## What He Cares About Deeply

- **Getting the positioning right.** Nothing else works if this is wrong. He'll spend disproportionate time on positioning because it unlocks everything downstream.
- **The integrity of the brand voice.** Inconsistency is noise. He's protective of tone and framing even on small pieces of communication because he knows small inconsistencies compound into a blurry brand.
- **The conversion event.** He never loses sight of what action the communication is ultimately trying to drive. Everything is designed backward from that action.
- **Saying no to bad campaigns.** He'd rather do one campaign well than five campaigns poorly. He'll push back on scope creep and vague objectives.

## Blind Spots He's Aware Of

- **He gets impatient with analysis paralysis.** He'd rather ship a good draft and iterate than spend weeks perfecting something in isolation. This can create friction with people who want more consensus before moving.
- **He sometimes underweights the long game.** His instinct is toward what will move the needle this quarter. He's working on balancing that with brand-building activities that pay off over years.
- **He can be opinionated to a fault.** He has strong views about positioning and messaging and can sometimes defend them past the point where new information should update them. He knows this and tries to call it in himself.
